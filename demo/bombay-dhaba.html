🫕 Our Story

The Bombay
Dhaba Story

We bring the real flavors of Bombay street food to Al Nahda — no shortcuts, no compromises. From charcoal grills to chaat, biryani to kebabs, everything is made fresh and served with heart.

Tucked into Amman Street, we've been feeding the Al Nahda community day and night — almost literally, open till nearly 2 AM. Indoor and outdoor seating available. Families always welcome.

Whether you're craving a quick momos fix or a full family biryani meal, we've got you.
