06 Philosophy

MY APPROACH

⚡

Intensity & Structure

Every session is designed with purpose. High intensity, high repetition, high retention. Players leave training better than they arrived — physically and mentally.

🧠

Intelligence Over Power

Tactics win matches. I develop footballers who can read the game, make decisions under pressure, and exploit space before others see it.

📊

Data-Informed Coaching

GPS metrics, Wyscout analysis, heat maps, and pressing intensity data feed into every training design and match preparation cycle.

🌱

Holistic Development

A player's psychological resilience, nutrition habits, and off-pitch mindset are as important as their technical and physical attributes.
