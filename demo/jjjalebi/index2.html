The Story

Born on Mohammed Ali Road.

In 1947, Rafeek Shaikh set up a small sweet shop near JJ Hospital, Nagpada. His jalebis were different — darker, denser, made with mawa instead of maida. Mumbaikars didn't bother with names. They just said "JJ Jalebi".

The name stuck. So did the queue. 75 years and three generations later — same kadai, same recipe, same secret chutney. Now serving Dubai.

1947

First branch outside India. Al Karama, Dubai 🇦🇪
