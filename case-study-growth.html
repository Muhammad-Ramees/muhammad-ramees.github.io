Campaign Overview

Capturing local demand at the moment of intent

Touchline is a Dubai-based football training academy operating in a highly competitive local fitness market. Despite strong coaching quality, the business lacked predictable inbound demand and relied primarily on referrals and inconsistent promotions.

I led the website build, local SEO execution, Google Business Profile optimization, and Meta Ads setup to capture high-intent searches and convert them directly into WhatsApp and website inquiries.

The Challenge

  • No Search Visibility: The academy did not appear for high-intent football training queries in Dubai
  • Weak GBP Setup: Under-optimized Google Business Profile
  • Unstructured Inbound: Leads came without tracking or prioritization
  • No Sales System: Inquiries handled manually via WhatsApp

Growth Strategy

Local visibility first, conversion second

Local SEO & Google Business Profile

Focused on ranking for bottom-funnel searches and dominating Google Maps visibility.

Attribution & Transparency

Leads were handled manually without a CRM or sales team. Conversions were tracked via WhatsApp conversations and Google Business Profile interactions. Two inbound leads explicitly referenced ChatGPT as their discovery source.
