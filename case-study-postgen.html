Project Overview

An intuitive web tool that empowers academy staff to create professional sports posters in seconds

Touchline Sport Academy wanted to regularly celebrate their students' achievements with professional "Player of the Day" posters for social media and internal recognition. However, creating these posters required either hiring designers (expensive and time-consuming) or using complex software like Photoshop (which staff weren't trained in).

I developed the Poster Generator - an interactive web-based design tool that allows any staff member to create professional, branded posters in under 30 seconds. The tool went live at touchlinesport.ae/post-gen and has become an essential part of their student engagement strategy.

The Challenge

  • Expensive Design Costs: Paying designers $20-50 per poster wasn't sustainable for daily recognition needs
  • Time-Consuming Process: Coordinating with designers and waiting for revisions took 24-48 hours per poster
  • Technical Barrier: Staff lacked design skills and tools like Photoshop were too complex to learn
  • Inconsistent Branding: Different designers created varying styles, diluting brand consistency
  • Limited Flexibility: Couldn't quickly create timely posters for spontaneous achievements or events
  • No Customization Options: Templates from Canva didn't match academy branding and required manual adjustments

The Solution

  • Zero Design Skills Required: Intuitive drag-and-drop interface anyone can use within minutes
  • Instant Poster Creation: Complete professional posters in under 30 seconds from start to download
  • Brand Consistency: Pre-designed templates maintain academy branding across all posters
  • Real-Time Preview: See exactly how the final poster will look before exporting
  • Complete Customization: Upload custom backgrounds, adjust positioning, add effects, and personalize details
  • High-Quality Export: Download ready-to-print PNG files with consistent resolution

How It Works

Simple 4-step process for professional poster creation

1

Choose Background

Select from multiple preset professional backgrounds or upload your own custom design. Each background is optimized for sports academy branding.

2

Upload Images

Drag and drop team photos, player portraits, and academy logos. System automatically handles image processing and optimization.

3

Customize & Preview

Use interactive sliders to zoom and position images. Add player name, age group, date, and academy details. See live preview of final design.

4

Export & Share

Download high-resolution PNG poster ready for social media, printing, or digital displays. Instant download with one click.
