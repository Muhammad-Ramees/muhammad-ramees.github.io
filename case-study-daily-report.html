Project Overview

A comprehensive management platform built to streamline sports academy operations

Touchline Sport Academy was managing daily operations using multiple spreadsheets, manual calculations, and paper-based records. This fragmented approach led to inefficiencies, data inconsistencies, and consumed valuable staff time that could be better spent on coaching and student development.

I developed the Daily Report Software - a centralized web-based management platform that consolidates all daily operations including finance tracking, attendance management, admissions, and task coordination into one intuitive system. The platform went live at touchlinesport.ae/daily-report and transformed how the academy manages its operations.

The Challenge

  • Fragmented Data Management: Multiple Excel sheets for income, expenses, attendance, and tasks with no central system
  • Manual Calculations: Staff spending hours calculating daily totals, net balance, and generating reports manually
  • Data Entry Errors: Frequent mistakes from manual data entry and formula errors in spreadsheets
  • No Real-time Overview: Couldn't quickly see daily performance or operational status at a glance
  • Difficult Reporting: Creating reports for management required compiling data from multiple sources
  • Limited Accessibility: Data locked in local files, not accessible when staff work remotely or from different locations

The Solution

  • Centralized Platform: Single web-based system accessible from any device, eliminating multiple spreadsheets
  • Automated Calculations: Real-time calculations for totals, balances, and statistics - no manual math required
  • Dynamic Data Management: Easy-to-use interface for adding, editing, or removing entries with built-in validation
  • Comprehensive Dashboard: Full daily summary showing all key metrics on a single screen
  • PDF Report Generation: Instant generation of professional reports for full day or specific sections
  • Task & Notes Management: Track tasks by status (completed, ongoing, pending) with custom notes capability

Key Features

Comprehensive functionality designed for sports academy operations

Full Daily Summary

Consolidates total income, expenses, net balance, staff and student attendance, and task status on a single summary sheet. Get complete operational overview instantly.

Financial Tracking

Separate detailed sheets for income and expenses with automatic calculation of daily net balance. Track every transaction with categories and descriptions.

Attendance Management

Track both staff attendance and student batch attendance separately. View attendance rates, patterns, and generate attendance reports effortlessly.

Admissions & Trials

Manage new student admissions and trial sessions in dedicated sections. Track conversion rates from trials to enrollments.

Task Management

Organize daily tasks by status: completed, ongoing, and pending. Add custom notes and important remarks to keep everyone aligned.

PDF Report Generation

Instantly generate professional PDF reports for the full day or specific sections. Perfect for management reviews, audits, or record-keeping.

Dynamic Data Entry

Easily add, edit, or remove entries for income, expenses, staff, batches, or admissions with intuitive forms and real-time updates.

Data Validation & Security

Built-in validation prevents errors. Secure access controls ensure only authorized staff can view or modify sensitive data.
